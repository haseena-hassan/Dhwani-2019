Antara

Music is a language that has the power to unite people across the globe regardless of their culture, traditions, likes and dislikes. Antara - the second note in Hindustani classical music- celebrates this. Dhwani organises workshops and competitions annually for all the music lovers across the state.
Workshops will be held by eminent personalities in the field of music not only within the state but also across the nation. Workshops are held in all genres of music ranging from classical to contemporary. Dhwani in the past had associated with people like Gowry Lekshmi and so on to create a synergic effect that left the spectators in awe.
Competitions are also an integral part of Antara. Platforms are laid for individuals as well as groups to showcase their talents. Individual competitions include Carnatic as well as contemporary solo rendition, a Dhwani Idol competition to help you make a mark in the music scene in India and many more. Acapella , Unplugged and so on are some of the other events provided to make an impact.
